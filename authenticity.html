Certificate of authenticity

Authenticity & Provenance.

Each piece from In Situ Editions is numbered and accompanied by a certificate of authenticity. The origin, placement, and ownership history of every piece are recorded in an immutable, publicly verifiable register.

How it works

Three steps to a permanent record.

01

A unique identifier

Every piece bears a discreet engraved plate with a unique serial number — part of its physical identity, present for the life of the object.

Serial number · engraved
02

Recorded on the blockchain

The identifier, the piece's story, its original placement, and its ownership are registered on Polygon — a public, energy-efficient blockchain. The record cannot be altered or deleted.

Polygon · public ledger
03

Verifiable & transferable

The owner can verify authenticity at any time by scanning or entering the serial number. In case of resale, the certificate transfers with the piece — the new owner inherits the complete provenance record.

Verify · transfer · inherit
Why it matters

An object with a memory.

Resale value & traceability

Provenance is one of the principal determinants of value at auction. A verifiable, unbroken record of ownership — from atelier to current holder — is an asset that appreciates alongside the piece itself.

European product passport

The EU's Ecodesign for Sustainable Products Regulation (ESPR) will require digital product passports for most goods by 2030. In Situ Editions pieces are designed to meet this standard from the first edition.

Guarantee against counterfeit

A record on a public blockchain cannot be duplicated or falsified. Any piece that cannot be matched to an entry in the ledger is not a genuine In Situ Editions piece.

Programme in preparation

Honest about where we are.

The certificate programme is being built in parallel with the first production run. Pieces from the inaugural edition will be the first to receive their on-chain record. No piece is currently listed on the ledger — that comes with delivery. If you have a question about the timeline, write to us directly.
