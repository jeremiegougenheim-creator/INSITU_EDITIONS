Why us

We designed the rooms.
Then we designed the furniture.

In Situ Editions is not a furniture brand that approached hospitality. It's the opposite. We are an architecture studio that has been designing the most exclusive private spaces in Asia since 2011 — the Chairman's Club at Wynn Palace Macau, The Library at The Hong Kong Club (founded 1846), the Dragon Room at The Bankers Club — and we drew pieces specifically for those rooms.

For fifteen years, clients have asked us: can I buy that chair? The answer was always no. In Situ Editions is the yes.

We don't manufacture generic furniture that happens to fit a hotel. We design pieces born from real hospitality projects — and now we produce them for yours.

Every piece is produced by manufacturing partners in Hong Kong and the Pearl River Delta, selected over fifteen years for our own projects. Six weeks Ex Works. One week by air freight, three to six by sea. The same ateliers, the same quality control, the same project management that delivered the Wynn Palace Chairman's Club.

Production & delivery

Transparent lead times.

Order type Production Details
Standard collection (1–49 pieces) 6 weeks EXW Any piece from the 15-piece collection, in standard finishes and dimensions. No minimum order.
Volume orders (50–500 pieces) 8–10 weeks EXW Same collection, volume pricing applied. Dedicated production line. Quality inspection reports included.
Modified dimensions 8–10 weeks EXW Adjusting height, width, or depth of existing pieces. Engineering review included in quote.
Bespoke finishes 10–12 weeks EXW Custom fabrics, leathers, metals, lacquers. Sample approval process before production start.
Fully bespoke design 12–16 weeks EXW New pieces designed to your brief by In Situ & Partners. Prototyping, revision, production.
Shipping — Air freight + 1 week Priority. Door-to-door. White-glove. Insured. Ideal for urgent chantier deadlines.
